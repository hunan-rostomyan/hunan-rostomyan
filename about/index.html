About

Hello. My name is Hunan Rostomyan. I'm a software engineer at WriteLab, where I apply machine learning to problems in nlp.

I'm passionate about mathematics and programming.

Though I love to play with numbers, mathematics, to me, has little to do with them. Mathematics is a web of ideas and techniques for understanding nature. It is all that remains when everything inessential is stripped away from a problem.

Programming has as much to do with computers as math with numbers. Not much, that is. If history helps us understand the what was, mathematics: the what is, fiction: the what could be, programming allows us to understand the how.
